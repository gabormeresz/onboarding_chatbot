## 1. Working Hours and Home Office Policy

Employees are expected to work 40 hours per week.
Core working hours are between 10:00 and 15:00 CET.

### Home Office Policy

- Employees are allowed to work remotely up to **2 days per week**.
- Starting from **September 1, 2025**, the home office allowance is increased to **3 days per week**.
- Home office requests do **not require manager approval** if within the allowed limit.
- Exceptions beyond the limit require HR approval.

## 2. Vacation and Time Off

- Annual paid leave: **25 days per calendar year**
- Sick leave must be reported via the HR system before 10:00 on the first day of absence.
- Medical certificates are required for sick leave longer than 3 consecutive days.

## 3. Benefits and Compensation

- Cafeteria budget: **500 EUR / year**
- Private health insurance is provided after successful probation.
- Probation period length: **6 months**

## 4. Internal Communication

- Official internal communication channels:
  - Email
  - Microsoft Teams
- HR-related questions should be directed to the HR ticketing system.
