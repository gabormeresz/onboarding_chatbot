# Equipment and Hardware Policy

## Standard Equipment

Each employee is entitled to:

- 1 company laptop
- 1 external monitor
- Keyboard and mouse

## Additional Equipment

Additional equipment (second monitor, headset, docking station) can be requested if:

- The role requires it
- The request is approved by the manager

## Equipment Request Process

- Equipment requests must be submitted via the IT Support Portal.
- The request must include:
  - Equipment type
  - Business justification
  - Office or home delivery location

## Equipment Replacement

- Faulty equipment must be reported immediately.
- Replacement requests are handled by IT Support.
