## Home Office Questions

**Q: Can I work fully remote?**  
A: Full remote work is not supported by default. Exceptions require HR approval and are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

**Q: Can I change my home office days every week?**  
A: Yes, as long as the weekly limit is respected and team coordination is ensured.

## Probation Period

**Q: What happens during the probation period?**  
A: During the first 6 months, performance and cultural fit are evaluated. Either party may terminate employment with a shorter notice period.

**Q: Can I take vacation during probation?**  
A: Yes, vacation is allowed during probation with manager approval.

## Personal Data Changes

**Q: How can I change my address or bank details?**  
A: Personal data changes must be submitted through the HR system. HR does not accept changes via email or chat.

## Salary and Compensation

**Q: When is salary paid?**  
A: Salary is paid on the 10th working day of each month.
