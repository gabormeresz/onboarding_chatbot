# Employee Onboarding Guide

## Day 1 Checklist

New employees should complete the following steps on their first working day:

1. Log in to the company email account
2. Access Microsoft Teams
3. Read the HR Handbook
4. Read the IT Policy
5. Complete mandatory security training

## First Week Tasks

- Request access to required systems (Git, Jira, Confluence)
- Set up VPN access
- Schedule onboarding meetings with team members
- Review internal documentation

## Support During Onboarding

- HR-related issues: HR Support Portal
- IT-related issues: IT Support Portal
- For urgent issues, contact IT Support via Teams
