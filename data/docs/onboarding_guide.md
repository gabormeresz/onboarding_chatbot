# Employee Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the company! This guide outlines the key steps and resources to help new employees get started smoothly. Completing the tasks below ensures system access, compliance, and effective integration into the team.

---

## Day 1 Checklist

New employees should complete the following steps on their **first working day**:

1. Log in to the company email account
2. Access Microsoft Teams and verify contact details
3. Set up Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
4. Read the **HR Handbook**
5. Read the **IT Policy**
6. Complete mandatory security and compliance training
7. Confirm access to the HR system

> ℹ️ If any system access is missing on Day 1, contact IT Support immediately.

---

## First Week Tasks

During the first week, employees are expected to:

- Request access to required systems:
  - Git repositories
  - Jira / issue tracking
  - Confluence or internal wiki
- Set up and test VPN access
- Configure development or analytics tools (role-dependent)
- Schedule onboarding meetings with:
  - Direct manager
  - Team members
  - Assigned mentor or buddy (if applicable)
- Review internal documentation and team guidelines
- Understand team processes (stand-ups, sprint planning, reporting)

---

## Mandatory Trainings

The following trainings must be completed during onboarding:

- Information Security Awareness
- Data Protection / GDPR (if applicable)
- Code of Conduct
- Health and Safety (where required)

Completion is tracked in the company learning system.

---

## Equipment & Workspace Setup

- Verify that all assigned equipment is received and functional
- Report missing or faulty equipment via the IT Support Portal
- Set up:
  - Email signature
  - Calendar working hours
  - Time tracking (if applicable)

---

## First Month Expectations

Within the first month, employees should:

- Gain access to all required systems
- Become familiar with internal tools and processes
- Start contributing to assigned tasks or projects
- Participate in regular team meetings and feedback sessions

---

## Support During Onboarding

For questions or issues, use the appropriate support channel:

- **HR-related issues**: HR Support Portal
- **IT-related issues**: IT Support Portal
- **Urgent IT issues**: Contact IT Support via Microsoft Teams

If unsure where to escalate an issue, your manager or HR contact can assist.

---

## Onboarding Feedback

- New employees may be asked to provide onboarding feedback
- Feedback helps improve onboarding processes and materials
