# IT Policy – Internal Use Only

This policy defines the rules and responsibilities related to IT systems, accounts, security, and acceptable usage. All employees and contractors are required to comply with this policy to protect company assets and data.

---

## 1. Account and Access Management

- All employees receive a company email account and identity (SSO) on their **first working day**
- User accounts are created based on the employee’s role and the **principle of least privilege**
- Access to internal systems (e.g. Git, Jira, Confluence, cloud platforms) must be requested via the **IT Support Portal**
- All access requests require **manager approval**
- Access is granted for business purposes only and may be:
  - Role-based
  - Time-limited
  - Read-only or restricted

### Access Review

- Access rights are reviewed periodically
- Access is revoked:
  - Upon role change
  - Upon project completion
  - During offboarding

---

## 2. Password Policy

- Passwords must be at least **12 characters** long
- Passwords must include a combination of:
  - Uppercase letters
  - Lowercase letters
  - Numbers
  - Special characters
- Passwords must be changed at least **every 90 days**
- Reuse of the last 5 passwords is not allowed
- Passwords must not be:
  - Shared with anyone
  - Written down or stored in plain text
  - Reused across personal and company accounts

### Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

- MFA is mandatory where supported
- Disabling MFA is strictly prohibited without IT Security approval

---

## 3. VPN Usage

- VPN access is mandatory when accessing:
  - Internal systems
  - Private networks
  - Company-managed resources from outside the office
- VPN must be connected before accessing:
  - Git repositories
  - Internal dashboards
  - Administrative tools

### VPN Issues

- Temporary connection issues should be troubleshooted locally
- VPN problems must be reported to IT Support if not resolved within **15 minutes**
- Repeated VPN connection failures may indicate:
  - Device misconfiguration
  - Credential compromise
  - Network security issues

---

## 4. Software Installation

- Only **approved and licensed software** may be installed on company devices
- Local administrator rights are **not granted by default**
- Software installation or upgrade requests must be submitted via the **IT Support Portal**
- Requests must include:
  - Software name and version
  - Business justification
  - Duration of use (temporary or permanent)

### Prohibited Software

- Pirated or cracked software
- Unauthorized VPN or proxy tools
- Cryptocurrency miners
- Personal cloud sync tools without approval

---

## 5. Device Usage and Security

- Company devices must be used primarily for work-related purposes
- Devices must:
  - Use full-disk encryption
  - Have automatic screen locking enabled
  - Run up-to-date operating system and security patches
- Devices must not be shared with:
  - Family members
  - Friends
  - Unauthorized third parties

### Lost or Stolen Devices

- Lost or stolen devices must be reported **immediately** to:
  - IT Support
  - IT Security
- IT may remotely lock or wipe affected devices

---

## 6. Data Protection and Handling

- Confidential and personal data must be handled according to data protection policies
- Storing company data on personal devices or cloud services is prohibited
- Data must not be:
  - Copied to external storage without approval
  - Sent to personal email addresses
  - Uploaded to unauthorized services

---

## 7. Monitoring and Compliance

- The company reserves the right to:
  - Monitor system usage
  - Audit access logs
  - Investigate suspected security incidents
- Monitoring is performed in compliance with applicable laws and regulations

---

## 8. Policy Violations

- Violations of this policy may result in:
  - Access revocation
  - Disciplinary action
  - Legal consequences, where applicable

---

## 9. Policy Updates

- This policy may be updated to reflect:
  - Security requirements
  - Legal obligations
  - Operational changes
- Employees will be informed of significant updates via official channels
