## 1. Account and Access Management

- All employees receive a company email account on their first working day.
- Access to internal systems (Git, Jira, Confluence) must be requested via the IT Support Portal.
- Access requests require manager approval.

## 2. Password Policy

- Passwords must be at least 12 characters long.
- Passwords must be changed every 90 days.
- Password sharing is strictly prohibited.

## 3. VPN Usage

- VPN access is mandatory when accessing internal systems from outside the office.
- VPN issues must be reported to IT Support if not resolved within 15 minutes.
- Repeated VPN connection failures may indicate a security issue.

## 4. Software Installation

- Only approved software may be installed on company devices.
- Local administrator rights are not granted by default.
- Software installation requests require an IT support ticket.
